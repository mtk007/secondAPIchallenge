This data experiment looks at weather and pollution variables at four surrounding locales around Pike Township, in Northwest Marion County, Indiana *zip codes: 46077, 46228, 46254, 46274, 46274, 46278, 46211, 46234, 46268, 46275 and 46298*

The determinate data points are not intended to fully determine relationship trends of wind and pollution in Pike Township, but to provide a reasonable visual approximation of ingress and egress of general airflow and generalized particulate contaminants over the township. The available datapoints are graphically portrayed in their approximate relation to Pike Township.
